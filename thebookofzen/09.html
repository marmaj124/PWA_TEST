RITUALS

READING TIME:
3 MINUTES
09 / 14

After each achievement in the company, we do interesting stuff together as a company. New achievement goals are going to be introduced by Frederik constantly throughout the year. We would like to make the best of the future company culture. That’s why we are going to focus on bringing you as much joy from us as possible. Prepare for some crazy stuff in the future. You will love it!


Happy Day


Once every month or two, everyone in the company is called upon to do a customer support shift. We call it Happy Day. Talking directly to customers all day helps us realize what’s wrong, what’s right, and what’s utterly confusing about Zen Water. For the first few times you do Happy Day, you’ll have full support from others to help you write clear and correct answers to customer questions through the chat but then you’re on your own. It’s an adventure which is waiting for you!


Better Me. Better Zen Water.


During the month of your birthday, you will participate in a special kind of activity. You’ll receive a request during your birthday month asking you for the names of some colleagues you want to receive feedback from. Those people will receive a short survey (~10 questions) which asks them about various aspects of working with you. Finally, you’ll complete a self-evaluation that asks about your overall feelings about your day-to-day work, your long-term goals, and your satisfaction with how the company is being run. Once all your surveys have been completed, the results will be compiled in an easy-to-read report. You’ll receive that report which consists of honest feedback from each person you have chosen. All questionnaires consist of open-ended questions, as opposed to a 1 - 5 scoring scale. Answering this way takes more thought and time, but it results in a much more useful form of feedback! Your answers as a feedback-giver are never anonymous. Fortunately, we offer an emergency confidentiality option if you’re truly uncomfortable sharing your thoughts directly with the person you’re reviewing.
