GETTING
STARTED

READING TIME:
1 MINUTE
06 / 14

Getting started at Zen Water can be overwhelming. There are a lot of little details, weird slang words and an enormous number of problems to solve. That’s totally normal. Your colleagues are always here for you, so don’t worry to ask them if you need some assistance. Be in the Zen Water ONLY if you love changing the world and helping other people achieve amazing things. You won’t do tasks without seeing the results, don’t worry.


If there is a requirement to do something you cannot do, be honest, don’t try hard on solving that problem if there is zero chance of succeeding. Try to tell others to do it and learn how to delegate.
