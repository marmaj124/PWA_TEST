WHAT ARE WE
GOING FOR?

READING TIME:
5 MINUTES
02 / 14

What do we do?
We help people to become better human beings in every aspect of their lives.


How do we do it?
By creating healthy, tasty and practical products like Zen Sleep which are enabling people to improve themselves in an efficient and enjoyable way.


Why do we do that?
Because we believe that we can help the planet to be more healthy, productive, relaxed and less stressed, sleepless and hectic.


The value of Zen Water is equal to how much value Zen Water creates for others. The value of You to Zen Water is equal to how much value you create for Zen Water.


Focus on how we can create value in any situation
The most vital thing to a business is to remember what any business purpose is to create value. We will always focus on how we can create value & how much value we possibly can create for customers, followers & partners.


Eg. How can we get people to like our Instagram posts? How can we get them to follow our Instagram page?


We of course focus on how we can create the value they desire from our Instagram page. Instagram is an entertaining & inspiring platform so we make sure we entertain people in every post & that we inspire them as much as possible.


Understand that feelings change the world
Always have in mind which feelings are crucial in any situation. People act on emotions, they remember emotions & they are controlled by emotions. If we control emotions, we control people. How can we create as much of the desired feelings as possible?


Eg. We are an unknown company, started by a group of young people, selling a product no one has ever heard of. What are some of the most important feelings to create to embrace that obstacle? Trust, Knowledge & that we are experts in our are of field.


Be original, don’t copy
Inspiration is fine but let’s make the world a better place by creating our own, innovative and original thoughts!


We always do our best. Our work is our pride.
Good is not good enough. Perfection is insurance that we have done all we could possibly do. If something is done “okay” then we will use more time to go back to that thing and redo it, than if we would just have done it to perfection the first time. Perfection brings a calm mind & pride. You have to be proud of what you are doing.


Be confident & certain when communicating to externals
Be Confident about our product, be confident about our numbers, be confident about your knowledge and be confident about how much value you can create for the other person. The more confident and certain you are about something, the more will people trust & value what you are telling them.


Be honest and thankful
We can not improve our wrongdoings & ourselves if nobody has the gut, to be honest. Honest truth might hurt in the short term, but it will definitely improve in the long term. True honesty might be a tough pill to swallow, but it's for sure a better pill to deliver. In Zen Water, we encourage an honest culture.


Remember to say: Thank you for your honesty. Honesty means that the person still care


English is the language of Zen Water
For sure, you can talk to your colleagues about non-Zen Water stuff in Latin, Spanish, Danish, Portuguese or any other language. That’s fine. Not every one of us has the best English skills. That’s true. On the other hand, we are changing the world. We need to be heard, we need to be international, we need to practise our English skills as much as possible.


By communicating in English most of the time, we improve ourselves in our future blog posts, interviews or annual reports. At the end of the day, new and new colleagues will come from around the world to Zen Water. We need to prepare for that and make all internal processes and material exclusively in English.


Be independent and responsible for yourself
After speaking about shared values, here’s one that breaks away: Independence. We encourage independent thought and original thinking. We don’t ask everyone in the team whenever there is a problem to solve. You are always welcome to ask feedback, but you should see things with your own eyes, make your own calls, and offer thoughts, perspectives, ideas, and products that you think are right, not that others think are right.


Be a good human being
Do what feels right, never start arguments without a proper reason and care about everyone in Zen Water. It's all about the journey but only if it’s fun.
