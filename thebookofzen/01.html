ZEN WATER
IS YOU

READING TIME:
3 MINUTES
01 / 14

Everyone in the team at Zen Water represents Zen Water and no one else. When a customer gets a message response from Frederik on Instagram, Frederik is Zen Water. When Alexander gets positive feedback from Melissa that our product was awesome, Alexander is Zen Water. We always need to help every customer in a time of their need or confusion. That is what they are going to remember, the emotion and received value at that specific moment. When you represent Zen Water it's important that you represent & signal the values of Zen Water. There are no excuses here.


It’s also worth mentioning that joining Zen Water can be sometimes all-consuming. We’ve seen it happen. You dig Zen Water tasks, so you feel pressure to contribute, maybe overwhelmingly so. The people in Zen Water are some of the best and most ambitious people in the world, so the self-pressure to be extraordinary is real. But here’s the thing: stop it. Settle in. We’re glad you love Zen Water because we all do too, but at the end of the day, it’s a job that should give you joy. Do your best, collaborate with your team, write, read, learn, and then turn off your computer and turn on Netflix. We’ll all be better for it.


Anyway, we have to remind you that Zen Water is good sleep, so you have to be energized. Zen Water is healthy, so you have to appear healthy. Zen Water is the focus, so you have to seem focused. Only then you can become the person you have always wanted to become. You will for sure never convince somebody using Zen Sleep, if you seem tired, unhealthy & all the opposite of what we are trying to sell.
