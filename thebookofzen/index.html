Book of ZEN allows us to discover who we actually are as a company hungry for success. Do not think of this as a book. Think of it as a flashlight. Many companies have fumbled around in the dark long enough. We have chosen to go choose something bright and powerful to help us find a new way. What do we stand for? How should we work together? Demystifying those beliefs into a short format, the Book of ZEN makes them easily understandable and, most importantly, applicable. Making Zen Sleep our best product is a guiding principle, but we cannot easily improve what we haven’t articulated. So whatever version of this handbook you’re reading, you can be sure it’s not the last. Please do help keep it up to date! And by up to date, we're not just talking about misspellings and team changes. So many of these company handbook projects turn out to be ideal-case prescriptions rather than real-world descriptions. They cover all the ways people should act, not the ways they actually do. That’s a shame. Let’s not write a company book like that. Let’s keep it honest. If you’re reading this just after joining Zen Water, it’s particularly on you, actually. Please take advantage of that glorious, shiny ignorance of being new, and question things. If you want to suggest a change in this book, send an email with the suggestions to mm@zenwater.dk
