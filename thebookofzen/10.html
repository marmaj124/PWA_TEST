INTERNAL
SYSTEMS

READING TIME:
5 MINUTES
10 / 14

We use different tools for different things. Here is the list of all of them. We might change it in the future but don’t worry. We won’t be increasing the number of tools we use. Rather, we will do our best to reduce the number of tools we use as possible. You will get access to all of those tools soon.


Main tools
(You will use them daily)


Moon


It is the main hub for everything that has to do with Zen Water. It consists of 2 main parts:

  1. Dashboard - a hub with links to other side-tools we use
  2. The Book of Zen

Think about Moon like the first website you open if you want to work on Zen Water.


Mail system


We use Simply as our mail provider, but we use either Apple Mail or HEY mail (mac mail apps) as our email managing apps. We don’t use browsers for mails mainly because of the distractions it might bring.


DISCLAIMER: We don’t use emails for internal communication, only for communicating with external people. Why? Working on mail brings chaos and confusion, and you cannot find anything.


Around


This is a special software for working remotely. We know that sometimes it is hard to meet physically with your team. That’s why we have chosen Around as our communication platform of choice. It isn’t a tool for video calls as you might think. It is a tool that enables you to work with someone simultaneously without a need to meet physically.


Whereby


If there is a requirement of having a video call with external people, we use exclusively Whereby. Our whereby address is https://whereby.com/zen. We use this tool mainly because it doesn’t require any installation to use it.


1Password


Yay! You are not going to need remembering passwords anymore. Zen Water is a company of the future and passwords have been here for a long time. So we decided to make the process of logging in much easier. We use 1Password for auto-filling passwords and usernames and we share those data within the company.


Signal


If you would like to call someone within the company, always use Signal. Beware of using Messenger or regular telephone calls! Any misuse of this can have a huge price of giving our potential important data. Prevention for the future is the best way we can assure that no leak is going to be there. You can add Signal as one of the calling options on iPhone or Android very easily. If you don’t know how to do it, we will help you with that.


Side tools that only a few teams use
(don’t worry about them now that much, you know that they exist )


Active Campaign


Our email marketing and CRM platform of choice. We use it for managing our email and customer campaigns connected with Facebook, Google Analytics and Instagram and we use it for coordinating B2B deals as well. Alongside Shopify, these are the only places where we held customer data!


Shopify


Our webshop system of choice. We use it for tracking orders and for selling our product line to B2C customers.


Facebook Business Manager


Tool for Facebook and Instagram advertisements. We use it to reach as many people as possible with our message.


Google Analytics


Used for making user experiences more relevant and pleasant. Thanks to Google Analytics, we gather all the data regarding user behaviour on our website. Additionally, it is connected to Active Campaign, so all users are given the best deals via our marketing campaigns based on their behaviour on our website.


Hotjar


We use Hotjar as an addition to Google Analytics. It gives us more in-depth insights and recordings of user behaviour. We use this mainly for optimizing content serving, design and user experience on our website.


Pensopay


Our payment wall of choice. We use Pensopay for accepting credit and dankorts. It is connected to the Quickpay platform, which gives us the ability to give users an easy to use paywall.


Bold Subscriptions


Our subscription system of choice. If customers would like to save 20% on our products, they are going for our subscription-based deals. Bold Subscription Shopify app assures that those recurring payments are possible.
