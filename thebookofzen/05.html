WHERE WE
WORK

READING TIME:
2 MINUTES
05 / 14

From our office
Only if there is going to be a requirement to be together or if we would just like to be together, we will meet at our office (either physically or virtually). The best part of it is that no, you don’t need to be at the office like in other companies. We treat your time as good as possible so if you don’t want to, you don’t need to commute to our office at all (or if you live 1000 km from it).


From home
Home sweet home. All of us know that our home space is a place where we love to spend our time, it’s the only place on Earth designed by you, so it needs to be amazing, right? We are aware of your comfort and time as well. We know that, on average, people spend approximately 2 hours per day just commuting to the office. It's the 21st century. We need to adapt as well. Without any limitations, you can handle all the work from home with us!


From any other place on Earth
Working from home all the time isn’t everyone’s cup of coffee. Thus, in the pre-COVID-19 and likely post-COVID-19 vaccine era, lots of us choose to work from coffee shops or other third spaces like coworking spaces either in some of our time or a lot of our time. Great for a buzz of other people and much-needed caffeine of course, but please do mind our basic security rules and procedures to ensure that nothing leaks on to the cafe/coworking wifi (you can learn about our security practices later on).
